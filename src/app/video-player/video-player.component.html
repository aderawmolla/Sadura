About US

Sadura Food Processing Plant PLC is a sister company of KT Business Group. KT Business Group is one of the largest companies in Ethiopia with an increasingly diversified business portfolio ranging importing and distribution of IVECO Vehicles and Spare Parts, real estate development, Transport, Water Bottling, Fruit Juice Processing, Exporting of Agricultural products mainly Oilseeds, Black seeds, Green Mung and Fenugreek.

Sadura Food Processing Plant PLC was established in 2015 by Ethiopian entrepreneurs with a vision to be the largest FMCG consortium in Ethiopia that invests in human capital and contributes to the growth of the economy.
